Introduction to UX

About

This course is about knowing what you want to achieve when designing for User Experience. You will gain insight into what User Experience is and what factors to take into account when creating a great User Experience. When defining User Experience people tend to talk about three aspects: what experience is, the quality of the experience and the design of experience.

Training Intercultural Skills

About

This course will enhance the awareness and purpose in the acquirement of attitude, knowledge and skills that will enable you to behave in a way that is appropriate and communicate effectively when dealing with other cultures. Besides this you’ll have Study Coaching: you’ll get an introduction to the Dutch educational system and the way it is executed in The Hague University of Applied Sciences. You’ll have talks with your study coach. You’ll contribute to and participate in the training and in the community of your class and lecturers, in which you’ll get acquainted with your fellow students and Dutch culture. You’ll find a buddy and will participate in excursions.

Design Creativity

About

Creativity is a crucial ingredient of design. In this course we will explore what creativity means for the design process and how you can use creativity in this process. And since visualizing ideas, concepts and messages is an important skill to enhance your creativity and to cooperate with others in design processes, a significant part of this course focusses on developing your visualizing skills.

Research For Design I

About

UX designers are investigative designers. In order to have your curiosity effectively spark your inspiration and to make founded choices in your design processes, you need to build an understanding of research and to develop your research skills. In Research for Design I we will focus on basics of research by example of the method of User Trialling, in which observation and interviewing are combined to learn about users’ interactions with technology.
